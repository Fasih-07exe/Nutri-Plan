What is Nutri Plan?

Nutri Plan is a smart dietary planning application that helps users generate personalized meal plans based on their dietary preferences (vegetarian, non-vegetarian, ovo-vegetarian) and fitness goals (bulking or cutting). The app uses intelligent logic to recommend balanced meals, track nutrients, and ensure users stay on course with their health journey. Users simply enter their name, select a goal, choose a diet type, and click to generate a complete diet plan tailored to their needs.
